PRODUCTS

Surfrut is a leading Chilean processor of dehydrated apples – and one of the three largest dehydrated apple suppliers worldwide. The strength of our company includes an in-depth knowledge of the market, a constant quest for innovation and top food safety. Customer service is our priority, giving a personalized service to our customers, developing solutions to their specific requirements. The current tendencies of the market embrace our values for healthy products, which include organic and natural crunchy snacks.

ORGANIC

Surfrut’s long expertise in Organic farming and processing goes back to the early 90´s when we realized we wanted to become a sustainable company. We have our own fields of organic apples, and we also work with a select group of farmers. The fresh apples are cultivated in the best soils with eco-friendly natural techniques. Apples are manufactured with utmost care at our plant in many different types and shapes to be delivered to our customers all over the world.
